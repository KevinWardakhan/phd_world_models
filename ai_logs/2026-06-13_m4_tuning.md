## Discriminator redesign
- `D(x_t) = head(mu_fake.forward_features(x_t, t))`; `head` is a single
  `Linear(hidden_dim, 1)`. No separate network, no separate time embedding for D
  (t flows only through the shared mu_fake encoder).
- `opt_disc = Adam(head.params + mu_fake.params)`: the GAN loss updates the shared
  backbone jointly, while the TTUR DDPM eps-loss keeps updating mu_fake. Faithful
  2D analogue of DMD2's classifier head on the fake-UNet bottleneck.
- Guards: teacher frozen; during the generator step only opt_gen is stepped (stale
  head/mu_fake grads cleared by the next zero_grad); DMD surrogate evaluates
  mu_fake under no_grad.

## Search space and ranking
- ~11 trials over `lambda_gan` {0.02,0.05,0.10,0.20}, `disc_lr` {1e-4,3e-4,1e-3},
  `disc_updates_per_gen` {1,2,3}, `gan_t` band {0.0-0.5,0.0-0.7,0.1-0.6} + full-range
  baseline. Init from best M3 student/mu_fake, fake_updates=3, no regression,
  3000 steps each.
- Ranking (n=5000): reject modes<8 or entropy<0.98; sort by ED+MMD, off-support
  tie-break; reject off-support-only improvements that clearly worsen ED/MMD.

## Selected model and result
- Selected: `lambda_gan=0.05`, `disc_lr=3e-4`, `disc_updates_per_gen=1`, GAN t band
  `0.0-0.7` (trial `trange_0p7`). Copied into `configs/dmd2.yaml`; `./run.sh m4`
  refreshes `outputs/m4/`.
- n=5000 ranking: tuned M4 beats M3 on all three (off 0.160->0.145, ED
  0.0021->0.0011, MMD 0.0008->0.0004).
- n=2000 clean eval: off-support improves (0.158->0.137) but ED/MMD tick up
  (0.0059->0.0088, 0.0022->0.0029). The two evals disagree because the distances
  are ~1e-3 and noisy, so the M4-vs-M3 gap is within evaluation noise.

## What was checked / honest takeaway
- Smoke-tested `train_m4` (6 steps) and the tuning script (2-trial tmp config)
  before the full CPU sweep.
- **Discriminator never really learned:** total accuracy stayed ~0.47-0.53 and
  logits ~0 across every trial; DMD grad norm (~1-2) dwarfs GAN grad norm
  (~0.1-0.2). The redesign and t-band restriction help dynamics but do not make
  the critic strongly discriminative, because the DMD loss has already solved the
  toy. Honest conclusion: **M4 is approximately equal to M3** even with the tuned
  hyperparameters; the discriminator-not-learning problem was the target and the
  redesign + tuning did not fix it much. Kept the tuned config (sweep winner, small
  change) without overclaiming a GAN-driven win.
