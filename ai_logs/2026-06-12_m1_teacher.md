# 2026-06-12 - M1 DDPM teacher

## Goal
Train a small DDPM teacher (epsilon prediction) that maps N(0, I) noise to the
8-mode checkerboard `pi_1`, show multi-step sampling recovers it, and keep a
clean frozen interface for M2-M4. No DMD/DMD2, no M2 yet.

## Design choices
- Standard DDPM epsilon prediction, linear beta schedule, `T=1000`, `sigma_t^2 = beta_t` (Ho et al., 2020).
- Terminal noise `N(0, I)` coincides with `pi_0` (std 1.0); train on raw coordinates (toy is near unit scale, no normalization).
- Small MLP (128 wide, 4 layers, SiLU) with sinusoidal time embedding.
- Evaluation is sample-based; off-support is treated as a sharpness signal and the acceptance criterion requires it to beat the `pi_0` baseline (a continuous model cannot match hard square edges exactly).
